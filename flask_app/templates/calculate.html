If you get a paycheck every week, this is the one for you. It is important to note that when entering you data in the form below, we only need your take home (or net pay). This is the pay that you have access to when your taxes and other expenses are taken out.

If you get a paycheck once every other week, then this would be the selection for you to choose. Typically for people that get paid, on average, twice a month, or every two weeks.

If you find you do not fit in to any of the previously listed categories, you should probably choose this one. Now, it is still very important to notice that we need your net income, after taxes and other mandatory expenses are taken out. There is a very cool tool you can use to figure a lot of this stuff out, just click here. Once you figure out your Net Annual Income, just fill out the associated form, or you can divide the number you got by 12, and click the button labelled "Monthly" on the top right.
